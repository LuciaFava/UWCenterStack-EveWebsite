The Project Details

Safe

Eyes off the road, means eyes not detecting potential oncoming dangers. It is critical that EVE promotes safe driving by being able to be controlled with minimal distractions.

Intuitive

Our grandparents are able to quickly learn how to operate Android and iOS devices. Why should EVE be any different. In fact, it will not even have manual!

Cool

When customers see the EVE interface, their first thought should be "Wow! I want this in my car!"

Green

The efficiency of a vehicle is heavily impacted by how it is driven. Therefore, EVE should provide useful feedback to the driver to help them cultivate good driving habits.
